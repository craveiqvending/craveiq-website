FAQ

Common questions from location partners.

Does my business have to purchase the cooler?

No. Placement terms are discussed during the location review. CraveIQ owns and operates its equipment unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Who handles stocking and cleaning?

CraveIQ handles routine stocking, inventory adjustments, cleaning and standard servicing.

Can we request specific products?

Yes. Inventory can be customized and adjusted based on demand and customer requests.

Is revenue sharing available?

Revenue-sharing arrangements may be available depending on traffic, expenses, placement and the final written agreement.
